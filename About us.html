Discover Our Story


- Welcome to SQU Market, your exclusive marketplace designed for Sultan Qaboos University students. Our platform is tailored to meet the unique needs of students, providing a space for buying and selling services within our university community.
- Our idea was inspired by the success stories of platforms like OpenSooq and Fiverr. We aim to combine the convenience of local marketplaces with the versatility of freelancing platforms to create a one-of-a-kind space for SQU students.
- At SQU Market, our main objective is to support the entrepreneurial spirit of SQU students. We provide a platform where students can not only buy services but also showcase their own talents, kickstarting their small businesses and gaining valuable experience in the commerce world.
- SQU Market stands out by focusing exclusively on the SQU community. We offer a range of categories from food and electronics to fashion and training, ensuring a diverse and vibrant marketplace for our students. Our commitment is to create a seamless and beneficial experience for both service providers and customers.
